How to Settle Personal Debts Outside of the UAE

21 May 2014 /
By admin /
Comment: 12 /

If you are unable to return to Dubai or any other GCC countries to settle your personal debts – including loans, mortgages, and credit cards in your name – you can legally appoint an individual to:

I. Make the repayments to the bank on your behalf for the full amount due; or
II. Enter into negotiations with the bank to settle the outstanding balances for a percentage of the full amount due.

Once your personal debts are fully repaid or settled, the bank will issue a clearance to remove your name from police blacklist and/or Interpol list if they have already filed criminal complaints against you with local and / or international authorities.

It is essential to seek professional legal advice for solutions tailored to your unique situation.
